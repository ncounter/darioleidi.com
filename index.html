Music

I started playing the electric guitar at 15 years old. The beginning was all about punk from the '90s: Green Day, Sum 41, Offspring, Blink-182, Rancid, etc. Then I started studying music in a private school for about 10 years. The focus slightly moved toward hard rock and metal (Dream Theater, Angra, Opeth, Alterbridge, AC/DC, Iron Maiden, and so many others) and shredding guitar techniques too (Kiko Loureiro, Andy Timmons, Joe Satriani, John Petrucci, Eric Johnson, and many others too). After all, a fork on the the jazz path took place. And eventually everything got mixed up, so now I enjoy the most of them all to listen and to play as well.
